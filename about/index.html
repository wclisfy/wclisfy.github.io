About

I'm Willis Chun Lai Wong, a linguist currently pursuing an MPhil in Linguistics at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I also received my BA in Linguistics (First Class). My work sits in generative morphology and its interfaces with syntax and prosody.

Research interests

  • Generative morphology — Distributed Morphology, reduplication
  • Morphology–syntax interface — phase locality, labelling algorithm, linearisation, interface conditions
  • Prosody–morphology interface — syllable fusion, prosodic structure, ideophones
  • Cantonese morphology and phonology
