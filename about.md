<h1>About</h1>

<p>
  I'm <strong>Willis Chun Lai Wong</strong>, a linguist currently pursuing an
  MPhil in Linguistics at <strong>The Chinese University of Hong Kong</strong>,
  where I also received my BA in Linguistics (First Class). My work sits in
  generative morphology and its interfaces with syntax and prosody.
</p>

<h2 class="section-heading">Research interests</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Generative morphology — Distributed Morphology, reduplication</li>
  <li>Morphology–syntax interface — phase locality, labelling algorithm, linearisation, interface conditions</li>
  <li>Prosody–morphology interface — syllable fusion, prosodic structure, ideophones</li>
  <li>Cantonese morphology and phonology</li>
</ul>
